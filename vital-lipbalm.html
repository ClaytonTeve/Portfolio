Vital Lipbalm

Vital Lipbalm is a fantismal product I invented and designed. Vital has four flavored lipbalm that infuse vitamins and nutrients to give a boost. Health Wealth to boost the immune system, Quick Stick to wake you up, Calm Balm to help fight anxities and Focus Pocus to help keep you on task. The nutrients and ingredients played a big part in the design of each label and the names were fun to come up with.
